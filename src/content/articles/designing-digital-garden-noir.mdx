## Palette logic

Pure black creates harsh contrast over time. A blue-shifted near-black reduces fatigue while still keeping the page quiet.

## Accent roles

`#6C63FF` drives calls to action, `#00D9C0` marks AI-native interactions, and `#FF6B6B` handles urgency and progress.

## Typography system

The display face introduces shape and personality. The body face stays neutral. Code uses a clear monospace with strong symbol differentiation.
