## Constraint as a design primitive

When bandwidth is capped, every dependency has to justify its payload. That shifts architecture from feature accumulation toward selective activation.

## Why Astro sits at the center

Astro emits static HTML by default, which means the article page is readable before any client JavaScript arrives. Interactive pieces become isolated islands instead of page-level obligations.

## Dynamic features without dynamic bloat

The API surface stays narrow:

- `POST /api/ai/chat` for streaming article companion responses.
- `GET /api/search` for semantic and fallback full-text retrieval.
- `GET /api/views` for asynchronous view counts.

## Build-time AI

Summaries, embeddings, and related article recommendations are generated ahead of time. That front-loads the compute cost and removes it from the reader path.
